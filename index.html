    FIBC Jumbo Bag

    We are one of the prestigious supplier and manufacturer of Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBC) that are designed as per the need of various several small and large scale industries in India. FIBC Bags are more demanding in indian markets. We offer these Bulk Bags in various specifications.FIBC jumbo bags: Also called big bags, bulk bags or jumbo bags, are the ideal solution for the temporary storage and transportation of dry bulk goods. This includes powders, mineral ores, aggregates and other ingredients. FIBCs have a very low packaging weight and are safe to use and available in various different forms.
