Company Profile

About Us

A.M.Plastic Industries is one of the leading supplier & manufacturer of PP Woven Sacks, HDPE Bags, BOPP Bags, FIBC Jumbo Bags, Woven Fabrics and Tarpaulins for application of Chemical, Fertilizer, Food, Pharmaceuticals, Mining etc.Since our establishment, we have never compromised on the quality of our products. We work with the mission of perfection and believe that perfection is the blend of quality, innovation and technology.


Client Satisfaction is the primary aim of our organization. Our products range is well recognized for the user-friendly nature, reliability and optimum performance. A team of highly experienced professionals who allows us to offer customized solution to our clients manages our organization. By providing products based on their specifications, we achieved full satisfaction of our clients.

Our Vision

To develop the new products at competitive rates foreseeing the needs of the market and double fold supplies and production in the coming years.

Our Mission

Our main aim is to work hard to become the pioneer in the plastic packaging industry and deliver the best in class quality product & service to the customer, thus help them grow with the pace.

Our Values

DRespect. Professionalism. Perseverance. These are the core values which guide us in our daily work and in the way we do business.
