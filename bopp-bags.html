BOPP Bags

AMPI has gained the reputed position in the market as one of the excellent suppliers of BOPP Laminated PP Woven Sacks. These sacks are having high tensile potency and are competent of bearing loads. Our quality checker team tests these sacks on various parameters of quality in order to make sure that the products are free of any fabricating errors.


We deliver the assorted requirements of our customers in every possible manner, and engaged in offering a premium quality range of BOPP Laminated PP Woven Sacks. We are making the sack using the best quality material which is sourced from leading vendors. We are manufacturing the sacks in various sizes and specifications to meet the varied needs of the clients. Moreover the BOPP Laminated PP Woven Sacks are used for the packaging of salts, fertilizers and sugar. The provided sacks are stitched from bottom; the mouth part is kept open which is duly non-lamination. Moreover these sacks are printed with attractive designs and prints to enhance the overall look and it can be availed at industry leading prices.
